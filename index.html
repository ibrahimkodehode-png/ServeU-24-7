Who We Are

Founded in 2025, ServeU helps property managers and short-term rental operators scale with confidence by providing reliable, remote support agents available 24/7.

We take the pressure off in-house teams by handling guest communication and daily operational support with professionalism and care.

Built as a fully remote company, ServeU operates across time zones with structured workflows, clear SOPs, and dedicated agents trained in short-term rental operations.

Our results-driven approach emphasizes responsiveness, consistency, and continuous improvement so our clients can focus on growing their portfolios while we handle the details behind the scenes.
