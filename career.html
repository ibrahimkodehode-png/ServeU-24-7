About ServeU

ServeU provides professional, remote guest support services for property managers and short-term rental operators...

Responsibilities

  • Handle guest communication across short-term rental platforms (Airbnb, VRBO, etc.)
  • Respond to guest inquiries in a timely and professional manner
  • Assist with check-ins, check-outs, and general guest support
  • Escalate issues according to SOPs when needed
  • Follow established workflows and communication guidelines
  • Maintain clear and accurate records of interactions

Requirements

  • Previous experience in short-term rental support, property management, or customer service
  • Excellent written communication skills in English
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to follow procedures
  • Comfortable working remotely and managing time independently
  • Reliable internet connection and availability for assigned shifts

What We Offer

  • Fully remote work environment
  • Long-term collaboration opportunities
  • Clear workflows and ongoing support
  • Flexible scheduling based on coverage needs
  • Professional and respectful team culture
